General Hair Health Tips

Balanced Diet is Key

Ensure your diet is rich in **protein**, **iron**, and **B vitamins** (especially Biotin). These nutrients are the building blocks for strong, vibrant hair. Think lean meats, eggs, nuts, and leafy greens.

Gentle Cleansing

Wash your hair regularly, but avoid harsh, **sulfate-heavy shampoos** which can strip natural oils. Massage your scalp gently to stimulate blood flow, and always rinse thoroughly with lukewarm water.

Minimize Heat and Chemicals

Limit the use of **heat styling tools** (blow dryers, straighteners) and chemical treatments (bleaching, perming). When you do use heat, apply a heat protectant spray first.

Hydration & Stress Management

Drink plenty of water and practice **stress reduction techniques** like meditation or regular exercise. High stress levels are a common trigger for various hair loss conditions.
